Pembahasan  

Materi pada bab 2 ini menjelaskan tentang Caption. Pada halaman 20 kita diminta untuk melengkapi dialog yang membahas tentang caption yang ada pada halaman sebelumnya. Setelah itu kita diminta untuk mempraktekan percakapan tersebut di depan kelas.

Berikut adalah isi jawaban dari dialognya:

Dialog 1

A: Which ones do you think are good caption?

B: I like caption number four.

A: Why do you think so?

B: It plays words so interestingly. It’s clever. Which one(s) is  not good caption?

A: I think number 1 is not a good caption.

B: Can you explain why it is not a good caption?

A: The words are not put properly. I mean I like the picture of  the flowers, but the caption writer cannot make it become an interesting caption.

Dialog 2

A: Which caption do you like the best?

B: I like caption number 4. The font is so interesting  and the combination of black and white color provides a clear contrast. What about you, which  one do you like the best?

A: I like caption number 5. The yellow color againts  with the greeny nature background gives an interesting image.

B: I like it, too. The words also give a clear message  about the article to come afterwards.
