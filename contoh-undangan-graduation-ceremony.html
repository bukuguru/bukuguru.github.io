14. Pertanyaan tentang present continuous tense 1 (+) Flora is attending the graduation. ceremony (-) .......................... (?) ............................ 2(+) Surti ia giving a birthday present to her friend (-)........................... (?).......................... TOLONG DI JAWAB


[Different forms of Present Continuous Tense]

Jawaban

( + ) Flora is attending the graduation ceremony.

( - ) Flora is not attending the graduation ceremony.( ? ) Is Flora attending the graduation ceremony?

( + ) Surti is giving a birthday present to her friend.

( - ) Surti is not giving a birthday present to her friend.( ? ) Is Surti giving a birthday present to her friend?Penjelasan

Kalimat-kalimat di atas adalah Present Continuous Tense. Present Continuous Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan situasi yang sedang berlangsung saat ini.

Rumus Present Continuous Tense:

( + ) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + verb 1 (-ing) + object

contoh: I am going to the library

( - ) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + not + verb (-ing) + object

contoh: She is not doing her homework.

( ? ) To be (is/am/are) + subject + verb (-ing) + object + ?

contoh: Are you sleeping in class?

Semoga membantu! :-)

present continuous tense merupakan bentuk tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang berlangsung selama waktu tertentu pada waktu sekarang (present). 

Rumus Present Continuous Tense

Dalam penerapannya dalam kalimat, kamu perlu mengingat rumus di bawah ini.

Bentuk positif: Subjek + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + …

Bentuk negatif: Subjek +be (am/is/are) + not + verb (-ing) + …

Bentuk interogatif: Be (am/is/are) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?
