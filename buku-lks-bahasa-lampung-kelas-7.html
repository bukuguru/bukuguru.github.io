Jawaban:

1. The mother always thinks of Her children, before thinking about Herself

2. They have a son. His name is James.

3. The cat cleans its body by using its tongue.

4. This bag is not mine. This bag is hers.

5. The story is boring. Other people have nothing to do with it.

6. We are camping at the campsite now. We need to cook for food here.

7. Take care of her even though you are busy.

8. Sometimes, They can be so selfish. She only thinks about Themselves.

9. She shares hers (her sandwich) with her friends.

10. My father bought a bicycle for me yesterday. This white bicycle is mine.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Pronouns (kata ganti) utamanya Subject Pronouns (kata ganti subjek), Object Pronouns (kata ganti objek), Possessive Pronouns (kata ganti kepemilikan), Possessive Adjective (kata sifat kepemilikan) dan Reflexive Pronouns (kata ganti refleksif) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Subject Pronouns: I, you, he, she, we, they

Object Pronouns: me, you, him, her, us, them

Possessive Pronouns: mine, ours, yours, his, hers, its, and theirs

Possessive Adjectives: my, our, your, his, her, its, and their

Reflexive Pronouns: myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves and themselves.

26. jawab buku lks bahasa inggris kelas 8 hal 57 semester 2​


Narrative

            I have a bad experience which I still (1) regret up to now. It happened in the second semester of Grade Seven. I am really (2) into Science, especially Biology. Since I like it very much, I always receive very good (3) marks. The teacher is proud of me and often (4) praises me above the other students.

             Once I would have a Biology test. The day before the test, I had many assignments to do so I felt very (5) exhausted. As a result, I was reluctant to study for the test.

            I didn't want to receive a bad mark, so I (6) preferred to do a bad thing which I had never done before.  I cheated by writing the answers on a small piece of paper.

             On the D-day, I could do test easily because I just (7) peeped at the paper and then wrote the answers.  Unluckily, while I was copying the answers for the last question, my teacher was (8) standing behind me. She immediately took my paper and (9) disqualified me. I was very embarrassed and regretful.

           I understood that my teacher was disappointed too. My previous efforts on receiving best marks were (10) ruined. Everyone thought that I received good marks because I cheated. I hope you learn a lesson from my bad experience.

Pembahasan

Narrative (story) is any report of connected events, presented in a sequence of written or spoken words, and/or in a sequence of (moving) pictures.

The Structure of Narrative Text:

Orientation : introduces characters and settings or the scenes of the story (who, where, when and etc.) Complication : the main characters faces problems Resolution : the problem is solved, statement of moral values

Terjemahannya:

Narasi (cerita) adalah kisah atau cerita tentang peristiwa-peristiwa yang saling terhubung, disajikan dalam urutan kata-kata secara tertulis atau lisan, dan/atau dalam urutan gambar (bergerak).

Struktur Teks Naratif:

Orientasi: memperkenalkan karakter dan setting atau adegan cerita (siapa, di mana, kapan, dan lain-lain.)

Komplikasi: karakter utama menghadapi masalah -masalah

Penyelesaian: masalah terpecahkan/terselesaikan, pernyataan nilai-nilai moral

27. kak tolong jawab bahasa inggris kelas 8 semester 1 buku lks​


Jawaban:

Joan : Nada, do you think English is difficult?

Nada : I don't think so, there is no difficult lesson.

Joan : Excuse me, I don't quite follow you.

Nada : Haha, that's my words!

Joan : In my opinion, it is difficult because I am hard to do every task that is given by our teacher

Nada : It is because you are lack vocabulary. By the way, what do you think about the English schedule in our class?

Joan : It's good. There is no problem with that.

Nada : I don't think so, I believe if we do English is in the morning, it will be easier to do the lesson. I am hard to follow that schedule

Joan : But I think if we learn seriously there is no hard in it. it depends on us, right?

Nada : Yes, I think if we learn seriously, there is no difficult lesson, so it depends on us.

Joan : Yeah, I think so too.

(Juga, Nada itu seorang munafik karena mengatakan bahwa Joan "lack Vocabulary" ketika dia sendiri memiliki kosakata yang buruk)
