39. Kunci Jawaban Bupena Bahasa Inggris Kelas 8 Halaman 6 Chapter 1​


Berikut kalimat yang termasuk ke dalam kelompok asking for attention sesuai yang diminta pada tugas Chapter 1 halaman 6 Bupena Bahasa Inggris kelas VIII adalah:

Attention please.Excuse me.Please look at me!I’m sorry to bother you.Hey, do you know what?

Adapun kalimat yang termasuk ke dalam kelompok asking and giving opinions antara lain:

He’s a nice boy. Don’t you think so?What do you think of the meal?Do you think Mrs. Endang teaches us well?How do you feel about our new classroom?I don’t think so.

Sementara itu kalimat yang termasuk ke dalam kelompok checking for understanding antara lain:

With me so far?Are you with me?Do you understand what I’m saying?Do you know what I mean?Do you know what I’m talking about?

Terakhir, kalimat yang termasuk ke dalam kelompok showing appreciation to other speakers antara lain:

Wow! You are very diligent. Excellent! You did it very well.Your story is very interesting. I like it. You always come early and do all your homework.Your picture is beautiful. I like the color.Your handwriting is very neat.Pembahasan

Soal di atas dikerjakan dengan mencermati sebuah percakapan. Setelahnya, siswa diminta untuk mengidentifikasi mana kalimat yang termasuk asking for attention, checking for understanding, asking and giving opinions dan showing appreciation to other speakers.
