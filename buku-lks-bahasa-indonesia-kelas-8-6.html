13. no text

14.... his face and style look very vicious. The synonym of the word vicious is... (d) cruel.

Artinya: wajah dan gayanya terlihat sangat bengis.

Penjelasan: kata yang paling mendekati kata bengis/jahat/kejam adalah cruel.

Arrogant = sombong

Coward = pengecut

Greedy = serakah

15. I.... (b) wrote a letter yesterday.

Penjelasan: tanda waktu yesterday menunjukkan peristiwa di masa lalu. Kata 'wrote' paling tepat untuk bentuk simple past tense.

16. This morning, the birds (a) flew to its nest.

Penjelasan: 'this morning' merupakan tanda waktu yg berkaitan dengan peristiwa masa lampu. Kata 'flew' yang paling tepat untuk simple past tense.

17. Last night, I (d) dreamt about something scary.

Penjelasan: 'last night' merupakan tanda waktu yang menunjukkan kejadian di masa lampau. Kata 'dreamt/dreamed' paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat simple past tense.

18. They (c) didn't play football in the field just now.

Penjelasan: 'just now' merupakan tanda waktu yang menunjukkan kejadian yang sudah terjadi, Yang kalimatnya menggunakan simple past tense. Kata 'didn't' paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat negatif dari simple past.

19. Did your brother (b) get married last ten years?

Penjelasan: 'last ten years' menunjukkan tanda waktu kejadian di masa lampau. Kata yang paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat interrogative diatas adalah 'get'.

20. Our friends (d) were happy with their achievement in the competition.

Penjelasan: Kata yang paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas adalah 'were' di mana were digunakan untuk subject jamak dalam bentuk simple past.

Pembahasan

Simple Past Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan tindakan di masa lalu, dan telah selesai di masa lalu.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: yesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, this morning, just now.

BENTUK simple past

    (+) Subject + verb 2  

    (-) Subject + did + NOT + verb 1  

    (?) Did + subject + verb 1 .... ?

Contoh:  

She slept too late last night.I didn't see him yesterday.Did you take my umbrella?What did you do last weekend?

10. jawab buku lks bahasa inggris kelas 8 hal 57 semester 2​


Narrative

            I have a bad experience which I still (1) regret up to now. It happened in the second semester of Grade Seven. I am really (2) into Science, especially Biology. Since I like it very much, I always receive very good (3) marks. The teacher is proud of me and often (4) praises me above the other students.

             Once I would have a Biology test. The day before the test, I had many assignments to do so I felt very (5) exhausted. As a result, I was reluctant to study for the test.

            I didn't want to receive a bad mark, so I (6) preferred to do a bad thing which I had never done before.  I cheated by writing the answers on a small piece of paper.

             On the D-day, I could do test easily because I just (7) peeped at the paper and then wrote the answers.  Unluckily, while I was copying the answers for the last question, my teacher was (8) standing behind me. She immediately took my paper and (9) disqualified me. I was very embarrassed and regretful.

           I understood that my teacher was disappointed too. My previous efforts on receiving best marks were (10) ruined. Everyone thought that I received good marks because I cheated. I hope you learn a lesson from my bad experience.

Pembahasan

Narrative (story) is any report of connected events, presented in a sequence of written or spoken words, and/or in a sequence of (moving) pictures.

The Structure of Narrative Text:

Orientation : introduces characters and settings or the scenes of the story (who, where, when and etc.) Complication : the main characters faces problems Resolution : the problem is solved, statement of moral values

Terjemahannya:

Narasi (cerita) adalah kisah atau cerita tentang peristiwa-peristiwa yang saling terhubung, disajikan dalam urutan kata-kata secara tertulis atau lisan, dan/atau dalam urutan gambar (bergerak).

Struktur Teks Naratif:

Orientasi: memperkenalkan karakter dan setting atau adegan cerita (siapa, di mana, kapan, dan lain-lain.)

Komplikasi: karakter utama menghadapi masalah -masalah

Penyelesaian: masalah terpecahkan/terselesaikan, pernyataan nilai-nilai moral
