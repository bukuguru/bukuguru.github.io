Dimensi Tiga

Kubus

r = 8 cm

Jarak A ke C

= AC

= √(AB² + BC²)

= √(r² + r²)

= r√2

= 8√2 cm

Jarak D ke G

= jarak A ke C

= r√2

= 8√2 cm

Jarak H ke B

= HB

= √(HD² + DA² + AB²)

= √(r² + r² + r²)

= r√3

= 8√3 cm

__

X pada EC

GX ⊥ EC

EG = r√2

EC = r√3

Jarak G ke EC

= GX

= EC × GC / EC

= r√2 × r / r√3

= 1/3 r√6

= 8/3 √6 cm

__

O tengah BD

BD = r√2

BO = 1/2 BD = 1/2 r√2

Y pada FO

BY ⊥ OF

OF = √(OB² + BF²) = 1/2 r√6

Jarak B ke bidang ACF

= jarak B ke OF

= BY

= FB × BO / FO

= r × 1/2 r√2 / 1/2 r√6

= 1/3 r√3

= 8/3 √3 cm

29. Guru sedang menjelaskan matamatika didepan kelas


Jawaban:

"The teacher is teaching math in front of the class"

Penjelasan:

║INTERPRETATION-PRESENT CONTINUOUS║

Answer

The teacher is teaching Mathematics in front of classroom

Or

The teacher is explaining Mathematics in front of classroom

Discussion

Kalimat yang diajukan dalam penterjemahan termasuk present continuous.

Parts of speech :

The teacher (Guru ) = Subject

is teaching/explaining   ( sedang mengajarkan/menjelaskan ) = Verb

Mathematics ( Matematika ) = Object

in front of classroom ( di depan kelas ) = Adverb of place

Explanation

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSETenses ini digunakan untuk menjelaskan aktiftas/tindakan yang sedang dilakukan ketika dibicarakan. Pada umumnya, bentuk kata kerja-nya berakhiran -ing setelah tobe (is/am/are) untuk menunjukkan/menggambarkan aktifitas yang sedang berlangsung ketika dibicarakan.Keterangan waktu ( adverb of time ) sebagai identifkasi tenses ini yaitu now, right now, still, at the moment.

Pattern/ Rumus

(+) Subject+tobe(is/am/are)+verb-ing+O/C

(-) Subject+tobe(is/am/are)+not+ verb-ing+O/C

(?) Tobe(is/am/are) + Subject + verb-ing+O/C?

Example

(+) My mother is watering the rose flowers now.

(-) My mother is not watering the rose flowers now.

(?) Is my mother watering the rose flowers now?
